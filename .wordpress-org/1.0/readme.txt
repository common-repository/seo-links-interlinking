== Description ==

SEO Links Interlinking is a powerful plugin that helps you add internal links in your wordpress posts. Automate internal link building with ease! SEO Links Interlinking automates Internal Link Building in WordPress. It is a powerful plugin that integrates with Google and automatically adds internal links based on the suggestions directly from Google!
For make SEO Links Interlinking works you have to create an account in wpseoplugins.org and generate an API KEY. This can be done easily in settings page of SEO Links Interlinking. When API KEY is generated you have to perform a connection to Google Search Console (https://search.google.com), using Google Connect button in settings page or simply using Add Links button in SEO Link Interlinking Metabox inside post page.
From settings page you have 2 methods for generate API KEY, login and registration. Both of them perform a call to api.wpseoplugins.org using email, password, name and surname. All of your data is treated according to our privacy policy (https://wpseoplugins.org/privacy-policy/) and our terms and conditions (https://wpseoplugins.org/terms-conditions/).

== Installation ==

Using the plugin is easy. Install and activate the plugin. Then signup to receive an API key from the SEO Links Settings page. Then connect to your Google Search Console. If you did not verify your site, please follow the guidelines to Verify and add your site to Google Search Console here: https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080

Done. You can now go into any post or page, and automagically add links to your posts.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use the plugin? =

Simply activate the plugin, go to plugin settings page on the left sidebar "seo links interlinking" menu. create an account or login to your account. that's it!

now you can connect to Google Search Console in order to get information about your website. If you did not verify your Google account in order to receive data about your website, you can visit this guide to verify your site ownership to Google (https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/9008080).

once the plugin is connected to your site and search console, simply click on "add links" inside each post or page, to automatically insert internal links in your content.

if no links are added, you also have suggestions on your right sidebar "SEO Links" for keywords you can use in your content, to automatically create internal links. 

also, you have a "SEO keywords" tab, where you can find useful words you can use to optimize your content for search engines. together with Google average position for that particular suggested query.

= What is Google Search Console = 

It is a platform owned by Google that, once you've verified you are the owner of your website, will provide you with useful information on how your website interact with Google, what people sees after searching queries on Google and discovering your website.
